Bring Donations to the digital age.

Donating to nonprofits can be easier and more efficient.

Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin enable fast and efficient transfers of money. No credit card processing fees, no waiting weeks for wire transfers. Some nonprofits publish their bitcoin wallet addresses, but that information is scattered around the internet. I want to build a free, open, crowd-sourced directory linking charities to their Bitcoin wallets.
